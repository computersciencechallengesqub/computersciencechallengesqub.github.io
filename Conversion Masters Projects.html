Conversion Masters Projects

The following projects were created for a new undergraduate first year module and are now being offered as possible conversion masters projects. Projects with a strikethrough are currently being completed by other students. The projects are designed to be outside of the comfort zone for most students. The projects are managed using a more intensive supervision approach than would normally be used. Supervision makes use of personal diaries and in some cases job-like 9-5 working in the CSB makerspace (once or twice a week). This approach has produced dramatic results for the right students. Conversion masters students with high levels of initiative and who have experience debugging code and teaching themselves technical work have historically done well with these kinds of project. In previous years students with existing programming skills and/or mathematical or scientific backgrounds have done very well (high to mid firsts) e.g. Maths, Physics, Mech&Aero, Chemistry. If you feel this is a good match for you please select one or more of the projects and contact me (j.bustard@qub.ac.uk) so we can discuss the project before you submit your preference.
